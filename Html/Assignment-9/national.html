🧊 1. India Launches Cooling Action Plan Amid Climate Concerns

- The government has unveiled a National Cooling Action Plan to tackle rising temperatures and promote sustainable cooling technologies. - The plan includes: - Promoting energy-efficient air conditioning - Reducing greenhouse gas emissions - Supporting eco-friendly refrigerants - It aligns with India’s climate goals under the Paris Agreement and aims to reduce cooling demand by 20–25% by 2037. - This is especially relevant as India faces record-breaking heatwaves and increasing urbanization.
